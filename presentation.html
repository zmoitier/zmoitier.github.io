About Me

Since 2022, I am a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Maryna Kachanovska in the applied mathematics department of ENSTA Paris (France).

From 2020 to 2022, I was a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Rainer Mandel in the department of mathematics of the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (Germany). I was also a member of the Junior Research Group Nonlinear Helmholtz equations (AP2) within the CRC – 1173 Wave phenomena .

From 2019 to 2020, I was a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Camille Carvalho in the department of applied mathematics at the University of California, Merced (USA).

From 2016 to 2019, I was a PhD student under the supervision of Stéphane Balac and Monique Dauge in the numerical analysis IRMAR team at the University of Rennes 1 (France).

Research Interests

  • Helmholtz transmission problem
  • Resonances
  • High frequencies
  • Numerical computation for unbounded problem
  • Finite element method for eigenvalue type problem
