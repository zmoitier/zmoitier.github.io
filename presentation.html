About Me

Since 2020, I am a postdoctoral researchers in the department of mathematics of the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (Germany). I am also a member of the Junior Research Group Nonlinear Helmholtz equations (AP2) within CRC – 1173 Wave phenomena.

From 2019 to 2020, I was a postdoctoral researchers uder the supervision of Camille Carvalho in the departement of applied mathematics at the University of California, Merced (USA).

From 2016 to 2019, I was a PhD student under the supervision of Stéphane Balac and Monique Dauge in the numerical analysis IRMAR team at the University of Rennes 1 (France).

Research Interests

  • Helmholtz transmission problem
  • Resonances
  • High frequencies
  • Numerical computation for unbounded problem
  • Finite element method for eigenvalue type problem
